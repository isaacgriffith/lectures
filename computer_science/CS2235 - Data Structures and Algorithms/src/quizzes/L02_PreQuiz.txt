Lecture 2 Pre-Lecture Quiz

Q1: Which of the following is not a principle of Object Oriented Design identfied in Chapter 2?
    A. Modularity
    B. Composition
    C. Encapsulation
    D. Modularity

    Ans: B

Q2: Select all of the following that are Object-Oriented Design Goals
    A. Robustness
    B. Composition
    C. Adaptability
    D. Amortization

    Ans: A, C

Q3: Which of the following must be declared as thrown by a Method?
    A. Errors
    B. Checked Exceptions
    C. Unchecked Exceptions
    D. None of the Above

    Ans: B
